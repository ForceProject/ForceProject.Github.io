Known Issues

Here I will post any known issues with the Force app for iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.
If you run into any issues with the app please check here first; if it not listed below, please contact me by filling out the form below the list of known issues or email me here and I will work hard to have it resolved in the next update.

  • Issue: When editting the tiles and you tap a tile to edit its settings and finish, the dots that where pulsing have stopped pulsing or have disappeared.
    Temporary Fix: Do not worry about it you can keep editting as you did, but with a bit of difficulty.If this is bugging you tap done to stop editting, the dots should come back. After this, you may return to editting if you wish.
