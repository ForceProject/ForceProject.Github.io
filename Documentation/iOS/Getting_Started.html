Getting Started

Once you have downloaded the Force app, it is very quick and easy to start creating your very first controller, don't be fooled by the number of steps, once you are used to them, it will seem like second nature.
Follow the steps below to get started.

Launch Force

On the first launch, you will be greeted with a quick and simple tutorial screen.
Feel free to flick through it, as you can view it again later at any time.

Add Controller

Tap Add Controller to create a new controller profile.

Fill in form

Note that you can change everything later except for the Layout section.

Le new controller

Tap the a controller to go to its interface and to begin controlling or to edit it.

Tap Edit

Tap the Edit button on the bottom right hand side of the screen to begin editting the interface to your controller.

Tap +

Tap the + button at the centre of the bar at the bottom to add a tile to your screen.

Tap a tile

Tap on a tile to add it to your interface, or long press it to view its built in documentation.

Move tile

Tap and hold on a tile to move its position on the screen, release to place it.

Tile settings

Tap on a tile on screen to access its settings and change its Tag, Size, etc.

Edit tile settings

Change everything from the tile's Tag, Size, and Content to suit your needs.

Tap Save

Tap Save to save the changes to the settings you made on that tile.

Connect

Repeat the steps above to add and customise more tiles to your controller.
Then tap Done to save all the changes, then Connect to control your project.
